Limitations noted

Paige HER2 Complete is CE-IVD and UKCA-designated but does NOT have FDA clearance and is not commercially available in the US for clinical use. It infers HER2 status from H&E images alone (no IHC); clinical adoption is contingent on prospective concordance vs. ASCO/CAP-guideline IHC/ISH workflow, which has not been demonstrated in a Tier 1 source accessible at the time of curation.
